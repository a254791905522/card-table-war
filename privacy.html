Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026

Card Table War is a single-player offline iOS game. This policy explains how the app handles information.

No Personal Data Collection

No personal information is collected, stored, transmitted, sold, or shared by Card Table War.

Local Game Progress

Mission progress, settings, and gameplay state may be stored locally on your device only. This information is not transmitted to us or to third parties.

No Third-Party Tracking

The app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking tools, social login, account systems, or in-app purchase services.

Children's Privacy

The app is designed as an offline game suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Contact

For support, use the App Store product page or visit Card Table War support.
